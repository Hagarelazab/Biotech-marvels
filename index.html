Drug Discovery

Explore the pipeline from target identification to clinical trials.

Register
🎧 Recorded Sessions – Build Your Foundations
  • Drug Discovery & Development – From the first idea to approved medicines.
  • Drug Research – Techniques used to identify and evaluate potential candidates.
  • Drug Targets & Pathways – How drugs interact with proteins, enzymes, and signaling networks.
  • In Vitro Pharmacology – Laboratory methods for studying drug action outside organisms.
  • Biomarkers – Key indicators for detection, development, and personalized medicine.
🧪 Live Sessions – Learn by Doing
  • Malaria Case Study – Causes, life cycle of Plasmodium, and treatment strategies.
  • Pharmacology Experiments on Frogs – Classic experiments illustrating organ effects.
  • Introduction to the Gut Microbiome – Microbes in health, disease, and metabolism.
  • Plants as Medicines – Aspirin, quinine, morphine; nature’s role in modern medicine.
  • How to Discover a New Drug – Leads, preclinical research, clinical trials.
🎯 Learning Outcomes
  • Understand the entire drug discovery pipeline.
  • Identify drug targets & signaling pathways.
  • Explain the significance of biomarkers.
  • Apply concepts to real diseases & lab experiments.
  • Recognize the roles of microbiome & natural products.
